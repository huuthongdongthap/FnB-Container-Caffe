## Key Findings

### Market
- **Zero digital loyalty programs in Sa Đéc** — no chains, no apps, only paper stamp cards
- **200K population**, 15-20 cafés, 4 customer segments (Gen Z 35%, Office 30%, Premium 20%, Tourist 15%)
- **90% smartphone penetration**, Zalo is critical, no app download needed = competitive advantage

### Product
- **Phone-auth** → zero friction signup (no app, no email, no password)
- **Cashback** → real money back (2-5%), unique in Vietnamese F&B
- **Referral loop** → 100pts referrer, FIRSTORDER (20%) referee, dual-sided incentive
- **3 tiers** → Đồng (0), Bạc (500), Vàng (2000) — achievable in Sa Đéc timeframe
- **9 drink-only rewards** → aligned with 100% drinks menu

### Financials
- **Launch budget**: 2.7M₫ (out of 15M₫ marketing budget = 18%)
- **Loyalty cost**: 3.9% of revenue (cashback 3.1% + points 0.8%)
- **COGS**: ~35% → net margin after loyalty: 61%
- **Breakeven on Vàng tier**: Positive margin even at 5% cashback

### Risk
- Highest risk: Staff adoption (mitigated: signup bonus per employee)
- Lowest risk: National chain entry (Sa Đéc not on any expansion map)
- Probability of 12-24 month uncontested dominance: **90%**
