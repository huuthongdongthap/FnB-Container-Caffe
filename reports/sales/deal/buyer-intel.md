## 1. Demographics — Who Goes to Cafés in Sa Đéc?

**Market size**: ~110K urban core, ~200K metro area. Small-city dynamics — word-of-mouth is the dominant discovery channel. No national chains present (Highlands, The Coffee House have not entered). Competition is entirely local street-side cafés (10K-25K₫) and a handful of mid-range independents.

### Segment Breakdown

| Segment | Age | Income | % of Base | Loyalty Target |
|---------|-----|--------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Gen Z Explorer** | 18-25 | 3-7M₫/mo | 35% | Mass acquisition — Bạc tier starter |
| **Office Regular** | 28-40 | 10-20M₫/mo | 30% | High LTV — fast-track to Vàng |
| **Premium Dater** | 25-35 | 15-30M₫/mo | 20% | VIP upsell — Vàng tier by spend |
| **Tourist** | 30-55 | 15-50M₫/mo | 15% | Low-commitment — guest loyalty (no signup) |

### Segment Profiles

**Gen Z Explorer (35%)** — Students and early-career workers. Primary trigger: TikTok/Instagram content showcasing the container rooftop. Decision hierarchy: photogenic space > affordable price > drink quality. Pain point: "every café looks the same" — they chase aesthetic novelty. Highly social, visit in groups of 3-6. Price-sensitive ceiling: 55K₫ is their psychological max per drink. Smartphone-native; Zalo for messaging, TikTok for discovery, Instagram for identity projection.

**Office Regular (30%)** — Mid-career professionals working within 3km of the café. Decision hierarchy: quiet atmosphere > reliable WiFi > fair price > proximity to workplace. Visit solo or in pairs, often for morning coffee ritual (cà phê sữa đá) or afternoon client meetings. Pain point: no café in Sa Đéc serves the "third place" function properly — too noisy, no power outlets, no loyalty recognition. They value consistency over novelty. Facebook-dominant for local information; Google Maps for discovery; Zalo OA for business communication.

**Premium Dater (25%)** — Freelancers, IT workers, online business owners. Disposable income higher than Sa Đéc average. Visit for date nights, group gatherings, remote work sessions. Decision hierarchy: rooftop view > signature drinks > table booking > VIP treatment. Pain point: "no place to date that feels special without driving to Cao Lãnh." They spend on combos (129K₫+ for 2 people). Instagram-heavy; will book ahead via website if available.

**Tourist (15%)** — Day-trippers from HCMC/Cần Thơ visiting Sa Đéc Flower Village or Huỳnh Thủy Lê House. Decision hierarchy: photogenic location > Google Maps rating > bilingual accessibility > parking. One-time visitors unless experience is exceptional. Pain point: no café in Sa Đéc is optimized for tourists — no English menu, no photo zones, no tourist-specific offerings. Google Maps is their primary discovery tool; travel Facebook groups are secondary.

### Who Is NOT the Target

- **Elderly (55+)** — Prefer traditional street-side quán cóc (10K-15K₫ cà phê đá). Container aesthetic is alienating, not appealing. Loyalty program via phone is a barrier.
- **Price-purists** — Customers who view coffee as a commodity and will not pay >20K₫ regardless of experience. No loyalty program converts this segment.
- **Pass-through motorbike traffic** — Quick stop, no seating. Capture with delivery (GrabFood) or takeaway-only counter, not loyalty.

### Ticket Size by Segment

|---------|------------|-------------|-------------------------------|
| Gen Z | 35-55K₫ | 100-180K₫ (3-4 people) | 1.8-5.7M₫ |
| Office | 45-65K₫ | 90-130K₫ (2 people) | 7-17M₫ |
| Premium | 55-80K₫ (solo) | 129-300K₫ (combo/group) | 2.4-9.6M₫ |
| Tourist | 55-100K₫ | — | One-time: 55-100K₫ |

**Blended average ticket**: ~55K₫ (aligned with AURA SPACE data).

### Visit Frequency

| Segment | Frequency | Monthly Visits | Peak Days | Peak Hours |
|---------|-----------|---------------|-----------|------------|

```
         MON  TUE  WED  THU  FRI  SAT  SUN
```

**Implication for loyalty**: Push loyalty sign-ups during off-peak (10:00-14:00) when staff have bandwidth. Target peak-hour regulars with "skip the line" or pre-order perks as tier benefits.

### Seasonality

- **Tết (Jan-Feb)**: Flower season brings tourism surge. Tourist segment spikes 2-3x. Office segment drops (holiday).
- **Rainy season (May-Nov)**: Rooftop utilization drops ~60%. Courtyard/Ground floor become primary. Shift loyalty perks toward indoor-friendly rewards.
- **Summer (Jun-Aug)**: Student segment peaks (school break). Evening hours extend later.

## 3. Loyalty Adoption Factors

### What Motivates a Sa Đéc Customer to Join

| Motivator | Relevance | Segment Fit | Program Hook |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|--------------|
| **Immediate discount** | Very High | Gen Z, Tourist | FIRSTORDER 20% off — instant gratification |
| **Cashback (real money)** | Very High | Office, Gen Z | "Tiền tích lũy trong ví" — tangible, no mental math |
| **Status recognition** | High | Office, Premium | Tier names (Đồng→Bạc→Vàng) signal social standing |
| **Birthday perks** | High | All | 30-50% off birthday month — Vietnamese gift culture |
| **Free drink milestone** | High | Office, Gen Z | "Mua 9 tặng 1" — familiar mechanic from street cafés |
| **Exclusive access** | Medium | Premium | Early access to events, reserved rooftop table |
| **Staff recognition** | Medium-High | Office | "Chào anh/chị [tên], như thường lệ?" — personalization |
| **Gift/treat-a-friend** | Medium | Premium, Gen Z | Referral bonus points — aligns with "bao" culture |

### What Blocks Adoption

| Barrier | Severity | Segment | Mitigation |
|---------|----------|---------|------------|
| **Phone number privacy concern** | High | Office (30+) | Show benefit before asking — "Nhập SĐT để nhận ngay 100 điểm = 10K₫" |
| **"Too complicated"** | High | Gen Z, Tourist | 1-field signup (phone only). No password, no email required. |
| **"I don't come enough"** | High | Tourist, Premium (low freq) | Guest tier — earn cashback without signup. Upgrade prompt after 2nd visit. |
| **"What's the catch?"** | Medium | Office | Transparent earn/burn rates. No expiry on points (key trust signal). |
| **No smartphone / data** | Medium | Tourist, Elderly | Staff-assisted signup on café tablet. Physical card backup option. |
| **"I'll do it later"** | High | All | Inertia is the #1 enemy. Staff must invite at payment. Give bonus points for signing up on first visit (scarcity: "chỉ hôm nay"). |

### Critical Adoption Levers for Sa Đéc

1. **Staff invitation is the highest-converting channel** — In a small city, personal interaction outweighs digital prompts. Train baristas to invite at payment with a script: "Anh/chị có SĐT để tích điểm không ạ? Hôm nay đăng ký được tặng 100 điểm."
2. **Zalo OA integration** — Zalo is the WeChat of Vietnam. If loyalty sign-up auto-subscribes to Zalo OA, you get both a loyalty member and a retargetable channel in one step.
3. **Cashback beats points for communication** — "Bạn có 5,500₫ trong ví" converts better than "Bạn có 55 điểm." Display cashback value in VND, not abstract points, on every receipt.
4. **First-visit urgency** — The highest drop-off is between "I'll think about it" and "I forgot." Create a same-day-only signup bonus (e.g., +50 bonus points if registered before leaving).

## 4. Digital Readiness

### Smartphone & App Landscape

| Factor | Status | Implication |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **Smartphone penetration** | >90% among 18-45 in Sa Đéc | Digital loyalty is viable for 85% of target base |
| **Mobile data** | 4G widespread, affordable data plans | QR scanning, web-based flows work without WiFi |
| **App fatigue** | High — users resist downloading new apps | PWA/web-first approach is correct. Do NOT build a native app for loyalty. |
| **QR code familiarity** | Medium — used for payments (MoMo, bank transfer) but not yet for menus | QR ordering/loyalty needs 1-2 month adoption curve. Print QR on every table + receipt. |
| **Digital payments** | MoMo is #1, ZaloPay growing, bank transfer common | Integrate MoMo QR on checkout. Cash is still dominant (~70% of café transactions). |

### Zalo — The Critical Channel

Zalo is not optional in provincial Vietnam. It is the dominant messaging + business platform.

| Zalo Feature | Usage in Sa Đéc | AURA SPACE Application |
|-------------|-----------------|----------------------|
| **Zalo OA (Official Account)** | Businesses use for broadcasts, support | Send loyalty balance updates, birthday offers, event invites |
| **Zalo Mini App** | Growing — mini programs inside Zalo | Future: loyalty dashboard as Mini App without app install |
| **Zalo Pay** | Growing fast | Payment integration at checkout |
| **Zalo groups** | Neighborhood + interest groups | Word-of-mouth spreads through Zalo groups — referral codes shared here |

**Recommendation**: Loyalty sign-up should auto-subscribe to Zalo OA. Send a welcome message with point balance. Broadcast monthly: "Bạn đang có X₫ cashback, ghé AURA SPACE dùng nhé."

### Platform Affinity by Segment

| Segment | Primary Platform | Secondary | Loyalty Entry Point |
|---------|-----------------|-----------|---------------------|
| Gen Z | TikTok (60%) | Instagram (30%) | QR code on table → web PWA |
| Office | Facebook (50%) | Zalo (30%), Google Maps (20%) | Staff invitation → phone input |
| Premium | Instagram (40%) | Website booking (35%) | Booking flow → auto-enroll |
| Tourist | Google Maps (45%) | Travel FB groups (30%) | QR code at counter + staff invite |

### Technology Stack Fit

AURA SPACE's existing stack (Cloudflare Workers + D1 + vanilla JS PWA) is well-suited for phone-based loyalty:
- **Phone-as-identity** is correct for VN market (no email culture for F&B)
- **PWA** avoids app store friction
- **QR-code-to-web** flow works on all devices
- **SMS OTP** not needed — trust-based signup with bonus incentive is sufficient for café context

**Watch out**: D1 latency from Cloudflare edge to Vietnam. Test P50/P95 response times for loyalty lookup at checkout. If >500ms, cache tier/balance in KV.

## 5. Referral Behavior

### Do Sa Đéc Customers Refer Friends?

**Yes — but it's organic word-of-mouth, not incentivized.** The UX interviews confirm that "brings friends" and "told friends about it" are common behaviors, especially for Gen Z (group visits) and Office (colleague recommendations). However, no café in Sa Đéc currently has a structured referral program, so there is no local benchmark for conversion rates.

### Referral Triggers in Local Context

| Trigger | Strength | Example |
|---------|----------|---------|
| **"Đi uống nước không?"** | Very High | Gen Z inviting friends to hang out — the friend group is the unit, not the individual |
| **"Quán này đẹp, mày thử đi"** | High | Sharing Instagram/TikTok of the rooftop — visual proof is the referral currency |
| **"Có chỗ ngồi yên tĩnh nè"** | High | Office workers recommending to colleagues for meetings |
| **"Lên đây chụp hình đẹp lắm"** | Medium-High | Tourist → tourist or tourist → local host |
| **Colleague ritual** | Medium | "Sáng mai ra AURA uống cà phê không?" — morning coffee as team activity |

### What Incentive Works Best?

| Incentive | Appeal | Fit for AURA SPACE Program |
|-----------|--------|---------------------------|
| **Points for referrer** (100pts = 10K₫) | Medium | Good baseline. Low friction. Stack with tier multiplier: Bạc = 120pts, Vàng = 150pts. |
| **Discount for referee** (FIRSTORDER 20% off) | High | Strong. "Giảm 20% cho bạn mày" is easy to say. Must work on first order only. |
| **"Bao" mechanic** (referrer gets free drink after 3 referrals) | Very High | Aligns with Vietnamese culture of treating friends. "Giới thiệu 3 người → tặng 1 ly miễn phí." |
| **Dual-sided cashback** (both get 5K₫ off next order) | High | Simpler to communicate than points. "Bạn và bạn của bạn đều được giảm 5K₫." |
| **Tier upgrade shortcut** | Medium | "Giới thiệu 5 người → lên hạng Bạc." Works for status-motivated Office segment. |

### Recommended Referral Program Structure

```
REFERRER (người giới thiệu):
  +100 points per successful referral (stack with tier multiplier)
  + FREE drink after 5 successful referrals (milestone bonus)

REFEREE (người được giới thiệu):
  FIRSTORDER code = 20% off first order (max discount 15K₫)

TRACKING:
  Unique ref code per customer (e.g., AURA + first 4 digits of phone)
  Referee enters code at checkout → system links referral
  Referrer earns points AFTER referee completes first paid order
```

### Referral Communication Channels

| Channel | Format | Why |
|---------|--------|-----|
| **Staff prompt** | "Giới thiệu bạn bè nhé anh/chị — cả hai cùng được tặng điểm!" | Highest conversion in small-city context |
| **Receipt footer** | QR + "Mã giới thiệu của bạn: AURA1234 — chia sẻ để nhận điểm" | Physical reminder, low cost |
| **Zalo OA message** | After 2nd visit: "Bạn có mã giới thiệu AURA1234 — gửi cho bạn bè nhận ngay 100 điểm" | Re-engagement + referral in one message |
| **Instagram/TikTok bio** | "AURA SPACE Sa Đéc | Mã giảm 20%: FIRSTORDER" | Tourist + Gen Z discovery |

### Referral Economics

```
CAC via paid ads:           ~12,000₫ (FB/TikTok ad → walk-in)
CAC via referral:           ~12,000₫ (100pts to referrer = 10K₫ cashback)
                            +  2,000₫ (FIRSTORDER 20% on avg 55K₫ ticket = 11K₫ discount)
                            ─────────────────
                            ~12,000₫ total, BUT:
                            - Referred customers have 37% higher retention (industry benchmark)
                            - Referee gets first-order experience at lower risk → higher conversion to repeat
                            - Referrer churn decreases (locked-in by points balance + social commitment)
```

**Break-even**: A referred customer needs only 2 visits to become net-positive (first order with 20% off + second order at full price with cashback usage). This makes referral the lowest-risk acquisition channel for AURA SPACE.

### Anti-Fraud Notes

In a small city, referral fraud is low-risk (identity is social, not anonymous). However:
- Limit FIRSTORDER to 1 use per phone number
- Flag referral chains >10 from same referrer (unlikely organic in Sa Đéc)
- Require referee to complete a paid order before points are awarded to referrer

## Summary: Loyalty Program Segment Strategy

| Segment | Acquisition Hook | Tier Path | Retention Lever | Referral Trigger |
|---------|-----------------|-----------|-----------------|------------------|
| **Gen Z** | FIRSTORDER 20% + 100pt signup bonus | Đồng → Bạc (after 5 visits) | Free drink milestone, Instagram check-in bonus points | "Đi uống nước với tao" — group dynamic |
| **Office** | Cashback transparency (VND, not points) | Fast-track Bạc → Vàng (after 10 visits) | Morning ritual, staff recognition, birthday perk | Colleague morning coffee invite |
| **Premium** | VIP enrollment at first booking | Immediate Bạc, Vàng at 2M₫ spend | Reserved rooftop, event access, tier status | Date-night recommendation |
| **Tourist** | Guest cashback (no signup needed) | Upgrade prompt on 2nd visit (if they return) | Bilingual loyalty, photo contest bonus | Google Maps review + travel group share |

**Critical success metric**: 30-day loyalty sign-up conversion rate. Target: ≥80% of first-time customers. If <50%, the signup flow or staff script needs immediate revision.
