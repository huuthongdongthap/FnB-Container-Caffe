## 5. PHOTOGRAPHY & ART DIRECTION

### 5.1 Style Guide Chụp Ảnh
| Yếu tố | Hướng dẫn |
|--------|----------|
| **Thời điểm ưu tiên** | Golden hour (17:00-18:00) + Blue hour (18:00-18:30) + Night |
| **Tông màu** | Warm shadows, cool highlights — teal & orange color grade |
| **Aperture** | f/1.8 - f/2.8 cho bokeh neon, f/8 cho architecture |
| **ISO** | Max 3200 (giữ clean cho night shots) |
| **Lens** | 35mm (không gian) + 85mm (detail/portrait) + 16mm (aerial rooftop) |

### 5.2 Subject Priority
1. **Neon glow** trên bề mặt container steel (hero shots)
2. **Rooftop view** hoàng hôn/đêm với Hotel Thảo Trâm context
3. **Drink close-up** với neon bokeh background
4. **Architectural detail** — container joints, steel textures, staircase
5. **Human presence** — staff pouring, guests at rooftop

### 5.3 Post-Processing
- **LUT**: Custom "Aura Night" — lift shadows warm, crush blacks, gold highlights
- **Vignette**: Subtle, 20%, top-left origin (simulate neon falloff)
- **Grain**: Film grain 15% (add texture to clean digital)
- **Sharpening**: 70/1.0/0 (for architectural lines)
